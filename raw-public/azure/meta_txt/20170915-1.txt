App Service - North Europe
Summary of impact: Between 19:30 and 20:15 UTC on 15 Sep 2017, a subset of customers using App Service in North Europe may have received HTTP 500-level response codes, have experienced timeouts or high latency when accessing App Service deployments hosted in this region. Preliminary root cause: At this stage engineers do not have a definitive root cause. Mitigation: Engineers determined that the issue was self-healed by the Azure platform. Next steps: Engineers will continue to investigate to establish the full root cause and prevent future occurrences
