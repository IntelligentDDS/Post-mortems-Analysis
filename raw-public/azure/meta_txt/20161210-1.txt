HDInsight - North Central US
Summary of impact: Between 22:30 UTC on 09 Dec 2016 and 05:10 UTC on 10 Dec 2016, customers using HDInsight in North Central US may have received failure notifications when performing service management operations - such as create, update, delete - for resources hosted in this region. Existing clusters were not affected. Preliminary root cause: Engineers identified a recent change in backend systems as a possible underlying cause. Mitigation: Engineers rolled back the change to backend systems to mitigate the issue. Next steps: Continue to investigate the underlying root cause and develop steps to mitigate future recurrences
