Content Delivery Network connectivity
Summary of impact: Between approximately 18:30 and 20:50 UTC on 17 Apr 2018, a subset of customers using Verizon CDN may have experienced difficulties connecting to resources within the European region. Additional Azure services, utilizing Azure CDN, may have seen downstream impact. Preliminary root cause: Engineers determined that a network configuration change was made to Verizon CDN, causing resource connectivity issues. Mitigation: Verizon engineers mitigated the issue by rerouting traffic to another IP. Next steps: Engineers will continue to investigate to establish the full root cause and prevent future occurrences.
